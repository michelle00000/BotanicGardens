SINGAPORE BOTANIC GARDENS

Did you know?

  • The Singapore Botanic gardens was founded in 1859
  • It stands today with over a 150 years of history as a UNESCO World Heritage Site

The Botanic Gardens is a suitable place for people of all ages. Offering an array of vibrant gardens and rich educational experiences, visiting the Singapore Botanic Gardens will definitely be a trip worth while.
There are 4 entrance points (i.e. Tanglin Entrance, Tyersall Gallop Entrance, Nassim Entrance, Bukit Timah Entrance) located in close proximity to public transport, making entry to Singapore Botanic Gardens very convenient.
